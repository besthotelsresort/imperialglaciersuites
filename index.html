Our Story

A coastline retreat, 49 years in the making.

There has been a resort on this shore under our family since 1977. What started with a handful of rooms is now 200 keys across the estate, with two restaurants and two private salons, yet the pace has not changed: slow mornings, long lunches, unhurried nights.

We do not follow trends. We set a standard in our first season and have spent 49 years defending it, one detail at a time: the linen, the lighting, the length of a pour, the exact temperature of the pool at seven in the morning.

The measure we care about is loyalty. Around 68 percent of the people who book each year have stayed with us before, and many arrive in the same week, in the same suite, as they did a decade ago.

C. Sinclair
Director of the House
